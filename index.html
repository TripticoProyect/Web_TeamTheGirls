Patron Saint Festivities


In the community of Sauz Alto, the festival of San Isidro Labrador is celebrated with great devotion and joy.



In the community of Sauz Alto, the feast of the Sacred Heart of Jesus is celebrated with deep devotion and joy, reflecting their strong faith.



On November 15 and 16, a celebration of her great devotion is celebrated in the community of Sauz Alto in the Hermitage of the Virgin Mary.





It is a significant event in Mexico and other countries when the Virgin is celebrated in her honor for everything she has done while they also love the Virgin to celebrate her on her day.

Gastronomy


In the tall sauz community it is very common to find gorditas de carnitas when entering the tall sauz.



In Sauz Alto, they eat tamales during rosaries as part of a beloved tradition. These meetings bring families together.



In the Sauz Alto community it is very common to find the gastronomy of pambazos and enchiladas during the day or night.

Architecture


The building of the El Sauz Alto Church is one of the most revered within the community with its unique design.



The construction of these structures is made of pure superimposed stone and has now become the common land of the community.



The building of these auditorium structures is very large and is used for special events and is also part of the community ejido.

Places to Visit



A place to visit would be the church garden, as sometimes a small Saturday market with desserts is set up there and it is an ideal place to go out with the family.




On the other hand, we have the library, which is a quiet place where you can spend time doing homework or just go read a book.
